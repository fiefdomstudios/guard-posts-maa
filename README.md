# guard-posts-maa
Minecraft mod - adds new blocks and entities

Adds two new blocks - guardpost and archerpost, which, when placed, spawn a new entity.
"Militia", or swordsman, and "hunter", or archer, are the two new entities spawned by the blocks.
These entities remain on their spawn block looking out for hostile mobs.
Hostile mobs within radius are engaged, then the entities return to their posts.
Entities may be healed with certain foods.
Entities may have their equipment upgraded by offering them certain blocks.
Spawn block disappears when the entity perishes.
